A couple of years in to playing bass I found it hard to improvise on the spot. I'd be lying if I said this wasn't the case anymore! When I was jamming with people I'd look at what chords they were playing and have an idea but it was a guess. I find when I try playing by ear I'll get a few of the chords without the help of anything like Moises Transcribe etc. When in a band and we had a song I'd ask the chords and keep refining what I was playing until it seemed good.

But that's a band scenario. On my own, I have found if I want to improvise a few ways to do it. I like picking a drum on BeatBuddy, then a key, then chords a pattern like 251 145.

If I'm feeling adventurous, I might even play some guitar, percussion and vocal in cubase!
