Join me in my bass journey. I have been playing bass in my spare time for a decade. I dabbled with guitar when I was a teenager with riffs, songs and a few bass lines. In 2014 on holiday someone was playing nirvana and bob marley and it made me want to start playing again. But I decided to get a bass as I thought it'd be easier! Then became my love for bass! I realise the songs I'm drawn to have great bass lines. I am a programmer and I always wanted to create a music website.

Check out my transcriptions where I am adding songs I am learning by ear, these may not be perfect! Over the years I have struggled a bit with improvisation so I have shared how I improvise. I have a lot of pedals - some info in the gear section. The past year I have been dabbling into percussion. Learning theory ongoing. A practice jam area.

Musical influences. When I was younger I liked Thin Lizzy, Blur, Oasis, indie type stuff like Echo and the Bunneymen. I liked Nirvana, RHCP, Rage against the machine, Radiohead, Manic Street Preachers, NOFX, more heavier Metallica, Pantera, Kyuss, Tool. I started mellowing out and stopped listening to heavy music. When I started playing bass I listened more to the songs I was learning to play. I also like Spanish music, Ibiza chillout type with nice guitar and bass. Bands. I am not currently in a band. I was in one serious band Les Pantalons. The songs Cold Ground, Wooden Foot, Do you Believe, Bad thoughts are the recordings I played bass in. We played a few gigs. We're still friends and jam from time to time.
