Join me in my bass journey. My website touches on all things bass.

I have been playing bass in my spare time for a decade. I dabbled a bit with guitar when I was a teenager
and played bass for the first time with my brother's friend's bass he borrowed for a short time. When he gave
it back I remember still playing a few bass lines like Ash Uncle Pat on the guitar.

In 2014 when I went on a summer holiday someone was playing nirvana and bob marley and I thought when
I get home I'm going to start playing again. But I decided to purchase a bass as I thought it'd be easier!
Then became my love for bass and I cannot set it down, my heart longs for bass.
I realise now the songs I'm drawn to have great bass lines. Over the years I have struggled a bit with improvisation.
I am a programmer so I always wanted to create a website to help people in their journeys so I am finally starting to create my website.

Check out my transcriptions, how I improvise, the gear I have, percussion, some theory and jam area.

My musical influences. I like anything with a good bass line. When I was younger I liked Thin Lizzy, Blur, Oasis, indie type stuff like Echo and the Bunneymen. I then liked Nirvana, RHCP, Rage against the machine, Radiohead, Manic Street Preachers, NOFX, more heavier Metallica, Pantera, Kyuss, Tool. I started mellowing out and stopped listening to the heavier. When I started playing bass I suppose I listened more to the songs I was learning to play. I also like Spanish music, Ibiza chillout type with nice guitar and bass.

Bands. I am not currently in a band. I was in one serious band Les Pantalons. The songs Cold Ground, Wooden Foot, Do you Believe, Bad thoughts are the recordings I played bass in. We played a few gigs. We did jam again but we didn't keep it up with work commitments.
